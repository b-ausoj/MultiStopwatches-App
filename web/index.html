MultiStopwatches - Free Online Multiple Stopwatch & Timer App

Features

MultiStopwatches is a powerful, free online stopwatch application that allows you to run unlimited timers simultaneously. Perfect for time tracking, productivity management, sports training, cooking, interval training, and any activity requiring precise time measurement.

Key Features:

  • Multiple Stopwatches: Run as many stopwatches as you need at the same time, each independently controlled.
  • Lap Time Tracking: Record lap times for each stopwatch to track intervals and split times.
  • Group Organization: Organize your stopwatches into custom groups for better management of different activities or projects.
  • History Saving: Automatically save all your stopwatch recordings with timestamps for future reference.
  • CSV Export: Export your timing data to CSV format for analysis in spreadsheet applications.
  • Progressive Web App: Install on your device and use offline - works without internet connection.
  • Dark Mode: Easy on the eyes with built-in dark mode support.
  • Multilingual: Available in multiple languages including English and German.
  • No Registration: Start using immediately without creating an account or signing up.
  • Completely Free: All features available at no cost, no premium tiers or hidden fees.
  • Privacy Focused: All data stored locally on your device, no cloud sync or data collection.
  • Rename & Customize: Give custom names to your stopwatches and groups for easy identification.
  • Sorting Options: Sort your stopwatches by various criteria for better organization.
  • Cross-Platform: Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices including iPhone, Android, and Samsung - any platform with a web browser.

Use Cases:

  • Productivity & Work: Track time spent on different tasks or projects simultaneously.
  • Sports & Fitness: Time multiple athletes, track workout intervals, or monitor circuit training sessions.
  • Cooking & Baking: Manage multiple dishes cooking at the same time with different timers.
  • Education & Teaching: Time multiple students during tests or activities.
  • Events & Meetings: Track different segments of events running in parallel.
  • Gaming & Speedrunning: Record and compare multiple run times with lap tracking.

How to Use:

Simply visit the website and start creating stopwatches. Group them by activity, start and stop them independently, record laps, and save your results. Export your data when needed. No installation required, though you can install it as a PWA for offline access.

Browser Compatibility:

MultiStopwatches works in all modern web browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and Opera. Optimized for both desktop and mobile devices including iPhone, Android smartphones, Samsung devices, and tablets.
